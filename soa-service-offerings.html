SOA Service Offerings

Styrone Inc offers complete range of SOA services for your organization’s needs – including

  • SOA Strategy and Roadmap
  • SOA Assessment
  • Services Discovery and Enablement
  • SOA Governance
  • Establish SOA Competency Center


SOA Strategy and Roadmap  

Each client’s requirements differ in several ways, and so must be their approach to adoption of SOA. For many, it is not easy to determine where to begin, what should be the roadmap, and how to measure success during implementation cycle. Styrone Inc offers Strategy services. We work with internal client resources to come up with a robust document providing a clear roadmap and advice. Our strategic services also extend to those corporations that have attempted SOA implementations in some form or shape, but are frustrated for the lack of tangible benefits.

As a part of strategy and roadmap development, if required, we also undertake demo or a quick Proof-of-Concept implementations. Typically, engagement for Strategy Services can span from few days to few weeks, and we typically perform the following :

  • Interactions with client’s stake-holders and technical team to understand the client's needs, priorities and available resources. We also capture details regarding their current IT investments, planned IT investments, business directions, competitive landscape, etc.
  • Proof-of-concept, if and where required
  • Prepare a high-level document that outlines –
    - Business and technical requirements
    - Enterprise SOA vision
    - Services based Business Architecture
    - Solution approach, high-level roadmap, periodic checks and guidance
    - Resource requirement, timeline, milestones, budgetary costs
    - Business benefits and/or RoI

SOA Assessment

SOA adoption is a long and continuous journey. It is important to ensure the journey is on right track, to reach the desired business benefits. Styrone Inc offers Assessment services to help organizations assess their SOA maturity. We assess SOA compliance of various projects against the maturity model.

Services Discovery and Enablement

Our custom development activities in SOA involve identifying set of services from existing IT systems – using both top-down and bottom-up approaches. With experience from earlier engagements, Styrone Inc has developed and refined multiple processes, frameworks and methodologies to facilitate rapid and effective enterprise SOA adoption. Our services typically include:

  • Modernize legacy systems
  • Identify set of services using both top-down and bottom-up approaches
  • Build new services
  • Develop a registry to make the search and discovery of appropriate services easy
  • Build, Test and migrate composite systems
  • Engineer systems for performance

SOA Governance

Styrone Inc helps clients develop or evolve an enterprise-wide governance model to transform the company into Plug-and-Play enterprise. Our services typically include:

  • Assess the existing IT Governance model and define SOA governance model
  • Define standards, principles, processes and policies
  • Includes restructuring existing organizations to align them with the SOA governance process
