Service Oriented Architecture(SOA)

SOA is an architectural style that helps achieve tighter Business-IT alignment. It defines resources, spread over an enterprise network, which have standards-based services that are reusable, platform independent and easy to integrate, and that can be assessed with a common interface. Such agility helps to create new business processes or modify existing business processes.

Styrone Inc is your trusted partner as you attempt to transform your enterprise as agile and competitive. Styrone Inc helps enterprise components to expose functionality as a service which eases business process deployment across the enterprise. Composite processes can be assembled from individual service oriented components.
