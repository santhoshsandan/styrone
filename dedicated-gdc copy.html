Styrone Inc also specializes in setting up client-specific ‘Dedicated Global Development Centers’ that draw on resources from across the company to meet the specific IT needs of our clients. We dedicate each of these centers for a particular client, complete with processes and infrastructure compatible to that client. The concept of the Dedicated Centers is to work as an ‘extended arm’ of a particular client and offer the timely delivery of comprehensive IT tasks at competitive price-points. At any point of time, we would be doing several projects in these centers spanning from development, testing, maintenance, migration and even support. It is also not unusual to have some team from our client to be based at these GDCs for some period of time.
